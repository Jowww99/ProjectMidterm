About

Sonder is a personal music player project built entirely with the knowledge I’ve gained so far in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. What started as a simple experiment became a creative space where I could blend design, functionality, and sound into one experience.


This music player is more than just buttons and playlists — it’s a reflection of my growth as a developer. From writing the first lines of code to styling every detail, Sonder represents what I can build with passion and patience.


Whether you use it to relax, focus, or get lost in your own rhythm, I hope Sonder becomes a place where your sound feels personal.


This is just the beginning — and there's more to come.
